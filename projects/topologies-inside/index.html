Inside a flat 3-torus A little planet at the centre of a cube whose opposite walls are identified.
Fly toward a wall and you return through the wall behind you — the repeated
planets are the periodic images of this one cell. A faint gravity pulls you
toward the nearest planet; let go and you coast and curve in.
