From the inside — flat 3-manifolds

Each frame is one fundamental cell of a closed flat 3-manifold seen from the inside. A small planet sits at the centre; its periodic images recede through the identified walls — exact tiling, no portal rendering. Click any manifold to fly around inside it.

Click a manifold to open the simulation. Use W/A/S/D to fly, mouse to look, E/Q to roll.
